Walk With Me

The youth theme this year is “Walk with me” (Moses 6:34), which is an invitation from Jesus Christ to more closely follow Him.

What does it mean to you to walk with Christ?
What things can you do in your life to come closer to Him and allow Him to bless you?

You’ll be walking through the park to find clues and solve puzzles, but try to also reflect on what it means to walk with the Savior.
