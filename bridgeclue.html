Bridge

In the scriptures, we learn that Jesus Christ is our advocate with the Father, and although we make mistakes and are not worthy to be with God, if we turn to Him, He will plead our cause before the Father. He will bridge any gap we have in our life and His righteousness and grace will allow us to pass through.

What do you think it will be like to return to the Father because of what Christ has done?
